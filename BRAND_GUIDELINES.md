## ‚úÖ Do's and Don'ts

### ‚úÖ Do's
- Use soft shadows and rounded corners
- Maintain consistent spacing (8px grid)
- Use category colors for organization
- Keep typography hierarchy clear
- Add subtle hover animations

### ‚ùå Don'ts  
- Don't use harsh shadows or sharp corners
- Don't mix font families inconsistently
- Don't use category colors outside their context
- Don't overcomplicate animations
- Don't ignore accessibility contrast ratios
